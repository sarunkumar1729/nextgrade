Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do Online & Distance Education Programs work? +

These programs let you learn from anywhere, anytime. You get access to lectures, assignments, and exams online, with flexible schedules. Ideal if you’re balancing work, family, or just prefer studying at your own pace.

2. What’s a Credit Transfer Program, and how can I use it? +

Credit Transfer lets you carry credits from one institution or course to another. Finished a diploma elsewhere? You might skip some classes and fast-track your degree with us—just check eligibility with our admissions team!

3. What makes the Apprenticeship-Embedded Degree Program (AEDP) unique? +

AEDP blends a full degree with paid apprenticeships. You’ll work on real projects with companies while earning your qualification—think of it as getting paid to learn, with zero student debt worries.

4. Are Skill Courses worth it? What do they cover? +

Absolutely! Our Skill Courses are short, focused boosts in areas like coding, digital marketing, or soft skills. They’re designed to upskill you fast—great for résumés or quick career pivots.

5. Can I switch between these programs? +

Yes, flexibility is key! Depending on your goals and eligibility, you can transition between programs like WILP, BVoc, or Online Learning. Contact our advisors to map out your path.

6. Are Online & Distance Education degrees as valuable as on-campus ones? +

Yes! Our accredited online and distance programs carry the same weight as traditional degrees. Employers value the skills and discipline you gain—plus, you can study from anywhere without missing out.
