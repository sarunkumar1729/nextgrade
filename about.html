About Us

At NextGrade , we redefine education with accessible, skill-driven, and industry-aligned programs. Registered under the Ministry of Corporate Affairs, our journey began with Career Line Academy in 2010. By 2021, we became an LLP and later transitioned into a private limited company, taking a monumental leap forward to expand our impact on a global scale.

Aligned with NEP 2020, we offer online and distance education, B. Voc programs, apprenticeship-embedded degrees, and credit transfer pathways, seamlessly integrating academics with industry through internships, vocational training, and mentorship.

Guided by Bodha Corporate Consultancy Pvt Ltd, we ensure academic excellence, business growth, and compliance. With a global presence, including in GCC countries, we empower learners with flexible, high-quality, and career-focused education.
